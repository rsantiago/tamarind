# Zero Marginal Cost Distribution

**Category**: Monopoly Strategy

## Description
A highly visible, floating "Badge" element on every free tier site that drives traffic back to the Tamarind landing page.

## Strategy
We will implement a **Two-Pronged Attack** for viral distribution on Free Tier sites:

1.  **The "Ghost Badge" (Bottom-Right)**
    *   **Visual**: A very discreet, semi-transparent small pill in the bottom-right corner. It should blend in, not scream.
    *   **Interaction**: On hover, it becomes opaque/active.
    *   **Role**: The persistent reminder. It catches the eyes of "Builders" who look at details.

2.  **The "Power Footer" (Bottom)**
    *   **Visual**: A clear, well-phrased Call to Action in the footer. Not just "Powered by Tamarind", but something compelling like *"Publish your own ideas with Tamarind"* or *"Built with Tamarind. Start yours."*
    *   **Role**: The standard attribution, improved for conversion.

Together, these ensure we capture both the casual scanner (Footer) and the intrigued power user (Badge).

## Goal
Turn every page view on a user's site into a potential impression for Tamarind.

## Impact
Viral growth loop. High visibility.

## Audience
Free tier users (billboards) and their visitors (leads).

## Effort
Low (CSS/HTML component).

## Implementation
1.  Design a "Pill" or "Badge" component.
2.  Inject it via the base theme layout for unlicensed/free sites.
3.  Ensure it is responsive and doesn't block content.

## Outcome
Consistent, free traffic generation from successful user sites.
