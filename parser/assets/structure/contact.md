# Let's Connect

We value human connection and thoughtful discourse. While Tamarind is a tool built on logic and code, its purpose is to facilitate the exchange of ideas between people.

## Support & Feedback

If you have found a bug, have a feature request, or just want to tell us how you are using Tamarind, the best place is our community channels.

*   **GitHub Issues**: For bug reports and technical discussions.
*   **Discord**: For real-time chat with other users and the developers.
*   **Email**: For private inquiries or partnership opportunities.

## Contribute

Tamarind is open source software. We believe in the power of community collaboration. If you are a Go developer, a designer, or a writer, your contributions are welcome.

Current areas where we need help:
1.  **Themes**: We want to offer more aesthetic choices out of the box.
2.  **Documentation**: Translating our guides into other languages.
3.  **Core**: Optimizing the build pipeline for massive sites (10k+ pages).

We look forward to hearing from you.
