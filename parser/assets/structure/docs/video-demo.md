# Video Embedding

Tamarind supports embedding YouTube videos directly in your content using a simple shortcode:

Syntax: `{{` `youtube VIDEO_ID` `}}`

## Example

{{ youtube M7lc1UVf-VE }}

This video is responsive and adapts to your theme's width!
