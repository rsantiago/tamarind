# Beyond the Stream

Social media operates as a **stream**. Content flows past you, ephemeral and temporary. If you don't catch it now, it's gone. It incentivizes hot takes, clickbait, and disposable thoughts.

A website is a **garden**.

## Tending the Soil

In a digital garden, content is not just "published" and forgotten. It is planted. You can return to old posts and update them. You can prune outdated ideas. You can graft new insights onto old structures.

## Structure

Tamarind's collection system (`articles/`, `docs/`, `notes/`) is designed to support this gardening approach.

*   **Articles** might be finished, polished essays.
*   **Notes** (concept) could be half-formed thoughts or research logs.
*   **Docs** are living manuals.

## Slow Web

We encourage the "Slow Web." Take your time. Write long form. Link deeply. Build a repository of knowledge that grows in value over time, rather than a feed that decays instantly.
