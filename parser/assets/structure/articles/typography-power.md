# The Voice of Text

95% of web design is typography.

When we strip away the images, the gradients, and the animations, what is left? The text. The information. The primary reason the user visited the site.

## Choosing the Right Pair

Tamarind ships with a carefully selected font pairing:

1.  **Playfair Display**: A transitional serif typeface. It has strong contrast between thick and thin lines, giving it a classic, "editorial" feel. It commands authority. We use this for Headings.
2.  **Inter**: A variable font designed specifically for computer screens. It has a tall x-height, making it extremely legible at small sizes. We use this for Body text.

## Measure and Rhythm

Good typography is also about math.

*   **Line Height**: We use a unitless line-height of `1.7` for body text. This gives the eye room to travel back to the start of the next line without getting lost.
*   **Line Length**: We constrain the content container to `740px`. This ensures lines are roughly 60-75 characters long, which is the optimal length for human reading speed.

By adhering to these typographic fundamentals, Tamarind makes your writing look professional by default.
